HexChat, your Windows IRC Client

Feb 27 2013
By Erwann Mest
  • irc
  • windows

You’re on Windows (or Linux) and you probably want to come back or/and use IRC. Well, HexChat is your boy. HexChat is an IRC client based on XChat, but unlike XChat it’s completely free for both Windows and Unix-like systems.

Everything is like XChat but better, more updated and free on Windows (other platforms too, for sure). Plus the theme system is really nice. Just download a theme and it will be packaged with an executable which allows you to easily change your HexChat colours.

Have a try!
